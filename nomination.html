BFSI Leadership Awards

Entry Requirement

  • Holds the title of CIO, CTO, CISO,CDO or equivalent senior IT Leader position
  • A member of top management
  • The applicant cannot be self-employed
  • The Application form needs to be signed by the Nominee and stamped by the organization.
  • Applicants must present accomplishments related to the applicant’s current position.
  • The final eligibility of the Participant will be subject to the discretion and approval of Jury.
  • Finalists will be expected to attend the Symposium in person.
  • Participants can upload up to three supporting documents (screenshots, photographs, references, project plan, demos etc.) - Max. Attachment size can be 15MB.

Evaluation Criteria for Indian BFSI IT Leader

  • “Vision and Leadership” The IT Leader must possess sound business acumen, astute foresight and the ability to lead, inspire and guide his/her organization to greater heights of growth, technology development and recognition.
  • "Knowledge and Experience", The IT Leader must have a sound understanding of all matters relating to the corporate regulatory compliance and reporting requirements of the company.
  • "Track Record and Accomplishments", The IT Leader's track record and accomplishments will be a key factor.

Evaluation Criteria for Indian BFSI IT Veteran

  • 30+ years of robust experience in IT domain
  • Served as CIO, CTO, CISO or equivalent senior IT Leader position for at least 10+ years
  • Sustained excellence and responsible for bringing the radical technology transformation into an organization
  • Strong track record of extraordinary Leadership and accountable in decision making of multiple departments.
  • Exceptional track record achievement, the judges will consider the corporate deals, rights issues etc, handled by the Nominee during the year under review.

Judging Process

The Jury Panel would evaluate based on the information provided in the Nominee Form and the supporting documents shared across by the applicants

  • The size and scope of responsibilities
  • Leadership and management effectiveness
  • Business value created by technology innovation
  • Engagement in industry and community endeavors.

Additional Information

  • Entries submitted without all requirements will not be considered.
  • By entering the BFSI Leadership Awards, entrants grant permission to reproduce the materials and information entered online in any magazine, book, promotional material, or online/digital format produced by Exito.
  • Failure to provide an interview or significant discrepancies between the entry and information provided after judging could result in forfeiture of the award.
